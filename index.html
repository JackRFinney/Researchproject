History and Milestones of Xbox

  • The Original Xbox

    • Public Domain. (2014, Winter). Original Xbox [Photograph]. Xbox. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xbox_(console)#/media/File:Xbox-console.jpg

    Nineteen years ago, the Original Xbox was released in 2001 after Microsoft's want for a gaming console to compete with the mainly Japanese dominated market. Originally named the DirectX Box was pitched by Kevin Bachus, Seamus Blackley, Otto Berkes, and Ted Hase to Bill Gates with the power and flexibility of a PC running on Windows 2000. In 2000 Bill Gates and Microsoft teased its release to the public at the Game Developers Conference. Microsoft needed to find games to compete with other businesses, so they bought the game developer Bungie for $30 million dollars and are asking for a new game, known today as Halo: Combat Evolved the first, first person shooter to be released to be completed in one year for its release. A year later Bill Gates and actor Dwyane "The Rock" Johnson reveled to the public the Original Xbox at the Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas. On November first the first Xboxes were sold and signed by Bill Gates himself with a $299 dollar cost for the new console. Halo was a huge success with the new console with one million copies sold in the first months of its release. It saw less success in Europe and Japan as compared to the North American release. In 2002 Xbox released Xbox live to the world. This allowed people to connect and play games with people around the world and friends alike. In one week, the service reached 150,000 users and the service began to grow larger. Halo 2 was released in 2004 sold more than 2.5 million copies in the first day, being the most successful lunch up to that point. In 2005 the Xbox 360 was released signaling to replacement of the Original Xbox and the new era in its history.

  • Xbox 360

    • Public Domain. (4 june 2014). Xbox 360 Pro [Photogragh]. Xbox. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Xbox_360_retail_configurations#/media/File:Xbox-360-Pro-wController.jpg

    After the success of the Original Xbox Microsoft wanted to continue to compete in the console market and cash in on more markets. In 2005 the Xbox 360 was revealed to the public through a viral marketing campaign on the website Hex 168.com. Launching on November 22nd, 2005, in the North American market. By December 2nd the release went worldwide with great success. At the end of the first year of launch it arrived in 36 countries with a price of $399. It sold 84 million consoles by the end of 2014. The Xbox 360 also doubled as a multimedia device, allowing movies and shows to be watched on the device. The Xbox 360 wasn’t without its problems though, a "Red Ring of Death" would occur and signal a general hardware error and would destroy the console. Microsoft soon offered a 3-year warranty for the issue. A new model called the Xbox 360 S improved all aspects of the Original Xbox 360. Then the Xbox 360 E was released in 2013 and improved even more of the first models of the Xbox 360. Five years after the release of the Xbox 360 Mircsoft released the Kinect motion capture system enabled games to be played by users using their bodies in the real world and helped the Xbox 360 to function longer. With the release of more Xbox game exclusives like Halo, Gears of War and Fable helped to entrench Xboxes position in the "Console Wars". On June 10, 2013 at E3 Microsoft released the Xbox One as Xboxes new console to the public signaling the end of the Xbox 360’s supremacy.

  • Xbox One

    • Public Domain (20 February 2014). Xbox one [Photogragh]. Xbox. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xbox_One#/media/File:Microsoft-Xbox-One-Console-Set-wKinect.jpg

    In November of 2013 at the E3 conference Microsoft released their third generation console the Xbox One, it can with many controversial features, such it being an always online device requiring internet unlike the previous consoles they released and making trading games with friend and stores almost impossible for the customer. It would also not be compatible backwards (that would later change). The Xbox franchise still hasn’t been able to recover after its horrendous reveal of the Xbox Oneas its announcement was considered a joke compared to the PS4 released around the same time. Luckily Xbox reversed most of its terrible decisions, but it was too little, too late as the console was never able to reach the prestige of the Xbox 360. The Xbox One also struggled to find exclusive games that where popular on the console with even its flagship game franchise Halo never reached its height again with Halo 4 not being able to compete with the main story and making as much profit as one of their spins off titles such as Halo 3 ODST and Halo Reach with Bungie leaving Microsoft and 343 studios given the responsibility of the Halo title. In 2014 Phil Spencer became the head of Xbox and greatly improved the console for Microsoft. In 2015 the Xbox released an update to consoles that would allow them to download and play games from the Original Xbox and Xbox 360. New games such as The Witcher 3, Fallout 4 and Halo 5 Guardians came as well to the console. In 2016 they released a new model called the Xbox One S and the Cortana virtual assistant named after the AI Cortana in the Halo Series. Later the Xbox game pass was released allowing a subscription to buy games on a monthly basis. In 2020 the end one the Xbox One had come with the Release of the Xbox Series X.

  • Xbox series X and S

    • Microsft (2020). Xbox Series X [Photogragh]. Xbox. https://img-prod-cms-rt-microsoft-com.akamaized.net/cms/api/am/imageFileData/RE4mRni?ver=a707&q=90&m=6&h=200&w=200&b=%23FF171717&o=f&aim=true

    Rumors of a new console had started all the way back in 2018 after Phil Spencer teased Project Scarlett, a new project for a new console. In 2019 at the E3 conference that year the Xbox revealed the new consoles Xbox Series X and S to the public with famous actor Keanu Reeves revealing the highly anticipated game "Cyberpunk 2077" during Xbox's game showcase. New exclusives such as Halo Infinite and Gears of War 5 were released with the new console (Halo Infinite was delayed until its release later this year on December 8, 2021) along with many more games on the game pass. The Original price of the Xbox Series X was $499 dollars releasing in 40 countries on the first day. Featuring backwards compatibility like the Xbox One. Xbox also released an app for computers so games could be played on PCs including exclusives such as the Halo: Master Chief collection releasing on the game platform Steam. Many of the games promised were delayed due to the Covid-19 pandemic. The Xbox Series X and S release was much better received by the public than the disaster that was the Xbox One release with people excited to play the new console that promised far superior graphics and operating systems. In March of 2021 Microsoft bought the game studio ZeniMax which owns Bethesda game studios which owns the Fallout, Elder scrolls and Wolfenstein franchises such as Skyrim and Fallout 3. With a promise of an amazing future Xbox many come back from its slump in gaming influence from the disaster of the Xbox One.

  • Xbox cloud gaming and the future

    • Xbox (2020). XCloud [Photograph]. Xbox. https://news.xbox.com/en-us/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/04/iPhone_iPad_Surface-2021_04_14-1920x1080-1.jpg

    The overall future of Xbox is unknown and uncertain, but Phil Spencer is slowly preparing the company for the future of video games and the potential of consoles becoming obsolete compared to PCs and Mobile gaming. Recently Xbox had released testing for the XCloud mobile gaming platform able to connect to Android, iOS, laptops, PCs and other devices so people can play anywhere at any time. This idea received very positive reviews with it having the possibility of revolutionizing the gaming industry. Xbox has been the only gaming console to release a monthly subscription to users and providing gaming on other platforms and devices separate from the Microsoft ownership. While Sony has stuck to the traditional console premise, Nintendo has also ventured into mobile gaming with the release of the Switch in 2017 allowing T.V. and mobile play. Xbox has been trying to work more closely with Nintendo, Steam and PlayStation to hold on to the gaming market and protect it from tech giants Amazon and Google and even have released cross-platform gaming among the consoles and PCs. There have been rumors around the gaming industry of Nintendo and Xbox working together after Phil Spencer teased Nintendo products in one of his livestreams. The Xbox game pass is a very important part of Microsoft’s plan for the future of gaming and the Xbox. Xbox has also promised to port PC games on to the Xbox One and upgrade it for the tons of people who still play on them. The future of not just Xbox but for gaming and gamers as a whole is looking very bright for a greater experience all around.
